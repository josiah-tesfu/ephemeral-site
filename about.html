ABOUT

2,000 origami butterflies.

20,000 minutes of folding.

A fleeting mural in public space.

This project explores the tension between beauty and ephemerality in the urban realm.

Moments and things we reach for but can't fully touch.

Both butterflies and paper are short-lasting in the moments they occupy. But with intention and presence, paper can become a butterfly, a butterfly can become a moment, and a moment can transform a space.

Concept and execution by Tesfu.

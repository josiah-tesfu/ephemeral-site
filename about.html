ABOUT THE PROJECT

1,650 origami butterflies.

10,000 minutes of folding.

The fleeting beauty and connection in the public realm.

Sometimes these are beautiful because they don't last. A conversation with a stranger who you'll never meet again. A butterfly that sits for a moment on a nearby flower.

But often, beauty and connection in public spaces are short-lived or never reached because our modern world doesn't prioritize them.

This installation invites you to contemplate the fleeting beauty and human connection present in our public spaces. Things we reach for but can't fully touch.

Both butterflies and paper are short-lasting in the moments they occupy. Paper can become anything, even a butterfly, but only with human intention and presence.
